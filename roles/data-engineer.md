# Data Engineer
**Remote (LATAM / Eastern Europe preferred) · Contract · 6 months**

---

Ventoux AI builds AI-powered decision intelligence for healthcare supply chains. Our platform turns fragmented procurement data, demand signals, and supplier performance into actionable insight — helping supply chain teams anticipate problems before they become stockouts. We work with organizations responsible for delivering essential medicines to hundreds of millions of people across Sub-Saharan Africa.

We're a small team tackling one of global health's hardest operational problems: making sure the right medicines reach the right places at the right time. Our technology is already working. Now we're scaling it for one of the world's most important supply chain operations — and we need someone who can make messy, fragmented data reliable.

## The Mission

We're partnering with a major UN agency and one of the world's largest philanthropic foundations to modernize supply chain decision-making across 100+ countries. Our client procures and distributes essential health commodities globally — reproductive health medicines, contraceptives, and life-saving supplies. Their data exists across dozens of country offices, reporting systems, and formats — but it's never been harmonized into a single analytical layer.

That's your job. You'll build the data infrastructure that makes our AI forecasting agents accurate and trustworthy. When a procurement officer in Copenhagen asks "which countries are at risk of stockout next quarter?", the answer depends entirely on the quality of data flowing through the pipelines you build.

The work matters. These supply chains prevent maternal deaths, treat HIV, and provide family planning to some of the most underserved communities on earth. Bad data means bad decisions. Good data means the right medicines reach the right places.

## Our Team

We're small, fast, and technical. Every team member owns their domain end-to-end. There are no layers between you and the product — you'll see your data pipelines powering real procurement decisions within weeks of building them.

You'll work alongside a full-stack engineer, a PM/training lead, and the founder (who built the entire codebase). You'll own the data layer. That means you're the authority on schema design, data quality, and pipeline reliability.

## Role

You'll own everything between raw source data and the analytical queries that power our AI agents. Health supply chain data in Sub-Saharan Africa is notoriously fragmented — inconsistent schemas, missing fields, different reporting cadences, multiple source systems. Your job is to make it clean, harmonized, and queryable.

The foundation is already built. We have a working DuckDB analytics engine, a schema harmonization framework for four countries (Rwanda, Kenya, Nigeria, Ivory Coast), and ingestion pipelines for CSV and structured data. You'll extend this to handle our client's data ecosystem — our client's procurement records, country-level consumption reports, supplier performance data, and demand planning inputs from systems like DHIS2 and OpenLMIS.

Our data stack centers on **Python and DuckDB** for analytics, with **pandas** for transformation, **Pydantic** for validation, **YAML-driven harmonization rules** for cross-country schema mapping, and **pytest** for testing.

## What You'll Do

- Own the DuckDB analytics pipeline — schema design, ingestion, harmonization, and query optimization across 100+ country datasets
- Build and maintain ETL/ELT pipelines for multi-source health supply chain data (CSV exports, DHIS2, OpenLMIS, ERP extracts)
- Extend the schema harmonization framework to map inconsistent country-level data into a unified analytical model
- Design data quality frameworks — validation rules, completeness scoring, anomaly detection, freshness monitoring
- Create bridge layers between raw source data and the forecasting models that power our AI agents
- Build monitoring and alerting for pipeline health and data freshness across country offices
- Document data schemas, transformation logic, and quality thresholds for eventual client handover
- Collaborate with the full-stack engineer on query performance and the founder on analytical requirements
