# Senior Full-Stack Engineer
**Remote (LATAM / Eastern Europe preferred) · Contract · 6 months**

---

Ventoux AI builds AI-powered decision intelligence for healthcare supply chains. Our platform turns fragmented procurement data, demand signals, and supplier performance into actionable insight — helping supply chain teams anticipate problems before they become stockouts. We work with organizations responsible for delivering essential medicines to hundreds of millions of people across Sub-Saharan Africa.

We're a small team tackling one of global health's hardest operational problems: making sure the right medicines reach the right places at the right time. Our technology is already working. Now we're scaling it for one of the world's most important supply chain operations — and we need someone who can build alongside us.

## The Mission

We're partnering with a major UN agency and one of the world's largest philanthropic foundations to modernize supply chain decision-making across 100+ countries. Our client procures and distributes essential health commodities globally — reproductive health medicines, contraceptives, and life-saving supplies. Their data exists but their tools don't keep up. Procurement teams learn about problems after they've already happened.

We're building an AI-powered intelligence layer that changes that. This isn't a proof of concept. It's a 6-month engagement to deliver production-grade analytical tools that procurement staff will use daily to interpret delays, identify supplier risk, and strengthen demand forecasting.

The work matters. These supply chains prevent maternal deaths, treat HIV, and provide family planning to some of the most underserved communities on earth. When the supply chain breaks, people suffer real consequences.

## Our Team

We're small, fast, and technical. Every engineer works across the full stack and ships end-to-end. There are no layers between you and the product — you'll see your code in the hands of procurement officers within weeks of writing it.

We believe the best solutions come from understanding how people actually work. You'll have direct exposure to the users and decision-makers who depend on what we build. Empathy for their constraints — fragmented data across dozens of countries, limited technical resources, high-stakes decisions under uncertainty — is what separates a good tool from one that changes how an organization operates.

## Role

You'll be one of four people on this engagement. That means vast ownership across the entire stack, decisions that shape the architecture for years, and direct accountability for what ships.

You'll take the existing Ventoux platform — a working AI agent system with 80+ tools, a computation engine, and a desktop application — and harden it for production use within our client's environment. This means adapting data schemas, building new integrations, hardening APIs, polishing the frontend for non-technical users, and ensuring everything runs reliably at scale across diverse country datasets.

You'll work directly with the founder daily. The codebase is well-tested (680+ tests), well-structured, and actively evolving. You'll be expected to move fast, make architectural calls, and own the quality of what you ship.

Our stack is purpose-built for AI-driven analytics: a backend foundation of **Python, FastAPI, and DuckDB**; a desktop application built in **React, TypeScript, Tauri, and Tailwind**; an AI orchestration layer using **Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, and Google Gemini** via the **Model Context Protocol (MCP)**; with **SQLAlchemy, Alembic, and Pydantic** for data modeling, and **pytest** for testing.

## What You'll Do

- Own production hardening of the platform for deployment within our client's internal environment — API reliability, error handling, performance, security
- Build and adapt MCP tools for procurement data schemas, supplier performance data, and demand planning systems
- Extend the DuckDB analytics pipeline for multi-country data ingestion and harmonization across 100+ country offices
- Ship frontend features in React/Tauri that make complex analytical outputs accessible to non-technical procurement staff
- Collaborate daily with the founder on architecture decisions, code review, and technical direction
- Participate in sprint demos and user feedback sessions with client stakeholders
- Write code that will be handed over to the client — clean, documented, and maintainable without ongoing vendor involvement
