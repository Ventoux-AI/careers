# Careers at Ventoux AI

We build AI-powered decision intelligence for healthcare supply chains — helping organizations anticipate problems before they become stockouts. Our technology supports the delivery of essential medicines to hundreds of millions of people across Sub-Saharan Africa.

We're a small, technical team tackling one of global health's hardest operational problems. If you're driven by mission and energized by ownership, we'd love to hear from you.

Send an email to **glenn@ventoux.ai** with:

- A brief intro — who you are and why this interests you

No cover letter needed. We read every application.
